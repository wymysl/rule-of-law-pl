## Notes on approximations

- **Lech** ‚ÄĒ the final `ch` is /x/ (the *Bach*/*loch* sound), which ARPAbet has
  no symbol for. `L EH1 K` is the nearest English fallback; `L EH1 HH` is an
  alternative if the voice handles a breathier ending.
- **ŇĀ (in PaweŇā, WaŇāńôsa)** is /w/, not /l/ ‚ÄĒ so "PAH-vew" and "vah-WEN-sah",
  never "Pah-vel"/"wah-LEN-sa".
- **ńė (in WaŇāńôsa)** is a nasal vowel […õŐÉ]; English has no clean equivalent, so
  /…õn/ (`EH N`) is the standard substitute.
- **ŇÉ (DoliŇĄska)** is palatal […≤] ("ny"); **zi (Ozimek)** is palatal [ Ď],
  approximated as `ZH`.
